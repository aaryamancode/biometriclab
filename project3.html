Release Agreement For AbHA dataset

Abnormal Human Action (AbHA) dataset addresses the commonly occurring abnormal actions in day-today lives of the elderly people. It covers five abnormal actions: chest pain (b) headache (c) fainting (d) falling backward (e) falling forward. It consists of 80 action samples containing depth videos with 640X480 resolution, 3D skeletal data for 20 skeleton joints per frame, and clean binary silhouettes by integrating depth and skeleton joint coordinates This dataset is captured by Microsoft Kinect v.1 cameras.

The database is released for academic research only, and is free to researchers from educational or research institutes for non-commercial purposes.
